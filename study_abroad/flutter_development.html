Master in Web Development FAQs

1.What is mobile app development?

Mobile app development is the process of creating software applications for mobile devices, such as smartphones and tablets.

2.What is the ‘Flutterverse’ Internship Program?

The Flutterverse Internship Program is an unpaid internship program for students and fresh graduates who are interested in learning how to build mobile apps with Flutter. The program is designed to give participants hands-on experience with Flutter development, as well as mentorship from experienced Flutter developers.

3.Who is eligible for the Flutter Internship Program?

To be eligible for the Flutter Internship Program, you must be a student or fresh graduate with a strong interest in mobile app development. No prior experience with Flutter is required, but you should have some basic programming knowledge.

4.What is the duration of the internship program?

The internship program spans 5 months, followed by 1 month of project training. After successful completion, you'll have the opportunity to join Plus IT Business Park at the start of the 6th month based on performance.

5.What is the cost of the internship program?

The cost of the program is 5000 INR per month, and there is a one-time registration fee of 2,000 INR. If you prefer to pay the entire amount at once, a discounted fee of 26,000 INR is available.

6.Are placement opportunities provided by Plus IT Business Park upon completing the internship?

Yes, Plus IT Business Park offers placement opportunities to successful interns. You can secure a 3-month paid internship opportunity with the potential to transition into a full-time role within the organization.

7.What kind of projects will I work on during the internship program?

You will have the opportunity to work on a variety of android/iOS projects, gaining practical experience and enhancing your skills in flutter development.
